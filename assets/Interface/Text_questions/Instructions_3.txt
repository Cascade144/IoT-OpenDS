OTHER DISTRACTORS:
\n
BOX DISTRACTION
During the driving a box falls in front of the users car. Objective is to avoid it to prenent Health loss.
\n
PEDESTRIAN DISTRACTION
There is a walking box acting as a pedestrian appearing in front of the car. Objective is to avoid colision.
\n
COLLECT DISTRACTION
User is asked to collect floating ball of either red or green color. The ball is collected by driving through it. If the user makes mistake, some Health is lost
\n
DARK DISTRACTION
Users screen is sometimes fogged for a short amount of time.
\n
TEXT DISTRACTION
User is asked to answer a question as best as he can. Driving is paused when the question is being answered.
